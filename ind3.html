Baga beach, Goa

Feel perky at the party hub of Goa, the Baga beach. This astounding beach is packed with vivid beach shacks and offers a spectrum of water sports. One of the most famous beaches in India, this beach characterized by its loud music, cheery ambiance and the exquisite views.



Best time to visit: October to January



Things to do: Watersports, relaxing at the beach shacks, partying with the lively night culture, shop at the nearby local markets

Radhanagar Beach – Havelock Island, Andaman

Being the best beach of Asia, the Radhanagar Beach is a sight to behold. The fine white sands and the light blue waters provide the perfect setting for rejuvenation. Commonly known as Beach No.7, this beach has a tourist footfall from around the world.



Best time to visit: October to February



Things to do: Take a walk on the pristine sands, relax and rejuvenate in solitude or with your loved ones.

Colva Beach, South Goa

One of the largest and the oldest beaches of Goa, the Colva beach has much more to offer than clean waters and virgin sands. Popular for rave parties, the beach is the perfect hangout place for the people of all ages.



Best time to visit: October to May



Things to do: Venturing into water sports, enjoying the nightlife, unwinding by the sea.

Varkala Beach, Kerala

Sunbathing and swimming with the siding of impeccable views, is what the Varkala Beach of Kerala promises. Also known to have medicinal and curative properties, the waters at this beach are pristine and the views picturesque.



Best time to visit: November to February



Things to do: Relax at the beach, visit the nearby Hindu Temple, and Take a dip in the curative waters at the beach.

Puri Beach, Odisha

Spectacular sunrises and sunsets are some of the best assets of the Puri beach in Odisha. White sands and clear waters of the beach further add to the glamour of the views. Known to be one of the most romantic beaches in India, this beach is ideal for holidays. You can also check out the famous places to visit in Puri while heading out to this beautiful beach.



Best time to visit: November to February



Things to do: Shop for handicrafts at the nearby markets, relax on the beach and enjoy the perfect sunrise and sunsets.
